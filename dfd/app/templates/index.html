What are DeepFakes?

Deepfakes are synthetic media generated through deep learning techniques, combining and superimposing images and videos. They raise concerns about misinformation and the need for advanced detection methods.

Technology Stack for Deepfake Detection

Our deepfake detection system leverages a powerful technology stack, including Flask for the backend API and TensorFlow for the detection algorithm. This ensures efficient analysis and identification of patterns within media content.
